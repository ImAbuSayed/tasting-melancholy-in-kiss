[RAW LYRICS]
[Hook]
I trace the shadows dancing on your skin
Where the midnight ends and my thoughts begin
There's a quiet sorrow in the way you sigh
But I find my heaven when our lips collide

[Verse 1]
The embers are glowing in the fireplace light
You pull my collar close to the fading night
I can feel the history written in your breath
A beautiful surrender, loving you to death

[Pre-Chorus]
Every single word you never have to say
Washes all the heavy miles of the world away
We're spinning in the quiet, tangled up and slow
Hold me a little closer, never let me go

[Chorus]
I'm tasting melancholy in a tender kiss
Lost inside the gravity of moments like this
Your memory is a river running wild and free
Washing out the sorrow that was drowning me

[Post-Chorus]
Tasting the melancholy, tasting the rain
Pulling me closer to ease all the pain

[Verse 2]
You trace the lines and the creases on my face
We're moving like shadows in an empty space
The coffee is cold but your hands are burning warm
You're the quiet shelter in my endless storm

[Pre-Chorus 2]

[Bridge]
I'd trade a thousand sunsets for this shadowed room
Just to breathe the fragrance of your sweet perfume
You whisper like a secret that I swore to keep
You got Saeed falling harder while the city sleeps

[Spoken]
[Whispered] (I feel every heartbeat you give me)
[Whispered] (Don't let the morning take you away)
